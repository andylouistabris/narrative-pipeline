# Screenplay Format Rules

## General

- Use scene headings clearly.
- Keep action lines visual and performable.
- Do not describe thoughts unless they can be acted, seen, or heard.
- Dialogue must reveal conflict, relationship, or decision.
- Avoid overlong exposition.

## Scene Heading

Use:

`內景／外景. 地點—時間`

or, when English format is required:

`INT./EXT. LOCATION - TIME`

## Action Lines

- Present tense.
- Concrete physical action.
- Avoid abstract commentary.
- Avoid camera directions unless specifically requested.

## Dialogue

- Character name on its own line.
- Parentheticals only when necessary.
- Do not overuse emotional instructions.

## Revision Rule

Fix story logic before polishing language.
