# Example: Revision Loop

## Step 1: Draft Output

Codex produces a scene draft.

## Step 2: Human Review

The creator checks:

- Did the scene obey Canon?
- Did the emotional turn work?
- Did the character say something out of character?
- Did the draft add unsupported backstory?

## Step 3: Multi-Model Critique

Other AI models evaluate:

- Structure
- Pacing
- Character consistency
- Dialogue
- Plausibility

## Step 4: Consolidated Rewrite Task

The creator or ChatGPT turns critique into a concrete task:

「保留場景結構，但重寫父子對話。減少說教，增加壓抑、閃避、爆發的節奏。不得新增回憶戲。」

## Step 5: Revised Draft

Codex revises only the assigned file.
